Hello Again

My name is Anna Tyler and I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design. I'm constantly learning new things everyday and love meeting people who are motivated and unafraid of sharing their ideas with the world. I'm a UX/UI designer and have learnt a lot of about concepts and theories of UX/UI, Web Dev, Type and Product design through studies and workshops, also I'm a bit of an experimenter and learning new stuff is my passion. I am very much a big-picture thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to development.
